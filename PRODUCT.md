# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective clients and recruiters visiting a design portfolio. They need to understand the work quickly, sense the author's judgment, and decide whether the designer is worth a deeper conversation.

## Product Purpose

This site presents Joseph Mugo's portfolio as a calm but distinctive brand surface. It should make selected work easy to browse while letting the interaction, typography, and pacing communicate taste. Success means a curious visitor can grasp the work, feel invited to continue, and leave with a clear impression of deliberate craft.

## Brand Personality

Simple stillness, calm, respectful, inviting, and a bit daring. The tone should feel thoughtful without becoming timid, premium without becoming cold, and curious without becoming noisy.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS portfolio patterns, flashy gimmicks, noisy card grids, over-explained prototype copy, heavy decorative chrome, and anything that feels like a raw Notion export. The interface should not feel corporate, trendy for its own sake, or too safe to remember.

## Design Principles

- Let typography and pacing carry the first impression.
- Make the work browsable first, then let curious visitors uncover more.
- Use daring moments sparingly so stillness remains the dominant feeling.
- Treat every project interaction as part of the portfolio's voice.
- Keep the interface respectful of attention: clear, quiet, and inviting.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive controls. Preserve keyboard access for project opening, closing, horizontal browsing, and card reading. Respect reduced-motion preferences for any animated transitions.
