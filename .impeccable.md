## Design Context

### Users
This is a design portfolio site powered by Notion content. Visitors are prospective clients, collaborators, recruiters, and design peers who need to understand the work quickly while feeling the author's taste and judgment through the reading experience.

### Brand Personality
Zen, premium, sharp. The interface should feel calm and deliberate, with enough editorial confidence to frame portfolio writing as crafted work rather than exported notes.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a refined editorial portfolio direction inspired by the restraint and precision of metalab.com and lovefrom.com. Light and dark modes are both acceptable, but the current pass should preserve the requested white base while avoiding generic SaaS styling.

### Design Principles
- Let typography carry the interface: strong hierarchy, generous measure control, and precise spacing.
- Keep the page quiet, not empty: use contrast, scale, and rhythm instead of decorative wrappers.
- Treat Notion content as portfolio prose, not a raw document export.
- Prefer premium restraint: fewer sizes, clear weights, calm color, and no noisy card chrome.
- Preserve readability for long-form case studies and notes.
